Taeoh Kim

Bio

I am a Researcher & Engineer in NAVER Cloud Corporation.

Before joining NAVER, I obtained my Ph.D. in Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University under the supervision of Prof. Sangyoun Lee.

I conduct research and development on technologies for Video AI (Video Understanding and Generation), and provide technical support for related services.

Also, we are always looking for an Intern interested in Video-related research. Please feel free to contact me.
